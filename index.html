Songqiang Chen

CSTAR & CS of Wuhan University, China · i9sch-remove-en@gmail.com

Songqiang Chen is now a master student in School of Computer Science and a member in Center of Centre of Software Testing, Analysis and Reliability of Wuhan University.

He used to be a contestant of Olympic Information (similar to ACM) and now a part-time junior coach of it. Over ten years of coding experience brings him a good understanding of programming and development, as well as a good knowledge of abundant programming languages such as C/C++, Python, Java, and PHP. It also makes him skilled at both software and hardware setup and maintenance. :)

His current research interest includes deep learning, intelligent software engineering, software testing, natural language processing, and computer vision.
