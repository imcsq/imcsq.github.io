Biography


Songqiang Chen is now a master student in School of Computer Science in Wuhan University and member in Center of Centre of Software Testing, Analysis and Reliability in Wuhan University. He is easygoing and good at thinking.

He used to be an Olympic Information (similar to ACM) contestant and now a part-time junior coach of it. Over ten years coding experience not only enables him to have a good knowledge of various programming languange such as C/C++, Python, Java and PHP, but also makes him skilled at equipment maintenance.

His current research interest includes deep learning, intelligent software engineering, software testing, natural language processing and computer vison.
