Songqiang Chen

CS & CSTAR of Wuhan University, China · i9sch-remove-en@gmail.com

Songqiang Chen is now a master student of School of Computer Science and Centre of Software Testing, Analysis and Reliability (CSTAR Group) in Wuhan University, China.

He used to be a contestant of Olympic Information (similar to ACM) and a part-time junior coach of it. Over ten years of coding experience brings him a good understanding of programming and development, as well as a good knowledge of abundant programming languages, e.g., C/C++, Python, Java, and PHP. It also makes him skillful at both software and hardware evtironment setup and maintenance. ;)

His current research interest includes deep learning (notably NLP and CL), intelligent software engineering (on code, artifact, and linkage), and software testing (mainly MT).
